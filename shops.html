Great Shopping

Trafford Center $$
Considered the biggest Shopping Center in Europe!Keep it for rainy days because, we assure you, one day is not enough to see everything! From big brands to themed restaurants, everywhere is worth a look. The location is quite far from the city centre: an hour journey by bus.

Arndale Shopping centre & Market Street $$
The place to go in town for your week-end shops. Near Piccadilly station and the shops continue all along Market street where you can find H&M, Primark, Paperchase,...

King's street and St-Anne's Passage$$$
Nice little street where no cars are allowed. The shops are luxurious but worth the walk. St Anne's Passage is a small gallery on the middle of the street with caffe's and shops. Quite hidden, make sure not to miss it !
