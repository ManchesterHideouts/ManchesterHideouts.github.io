Welcome!

Coming to Manchester for a short visit and want to get the low-down on the most exciting spots without having to trawl through a Lonely Planet guide? Or are you an Erasmus student who is just settling into Manchester for a term and are keen to discover the best of what this great metropolitan city has to offer?


If so, you've come to the right place! Manchester Hideouts aims to give you a quick summary of the best places in the city, whether you are looking for somewhere to stay, something to do or places to eat. We've done all the hard work so you don't have to. Our team have extensive local knowledge and we want to help visitors to Manchester find the hidden gems that locals would prefer to be kept secret. We hope you find the website useful and if you have any questions, please contact us below!
