Places to visit

Take a break from studying and escape (literally) with Breakout Manchester. Gather a group of your mates and test your team-work skills against the clock. Find clues and solve puzzles in order to escape before it's too late. Packed with brain-teasing challenges, this experience is guaranteed to be the most immersive hour of fun you'll have in a long time! Will you make it out of the Escape Rooms?
Jump to your heart's content inside the largest indoor trampoline park in Europe. Located near the Trafford Centre, Jump Nation promises to be a fun-filled day out for all the family. Try the Bubble Jump assault course, score a slam dunk with Dunk Nation or combine trampolines and skateboarding with the Jump Pipe! With something to suit adventurers of all ages, this venue offers a great way to burn some calories whilst having fun at the same time.
Tired of the rainy weather in Manchester? Why not brighten up your day with a trip to this chick cinema? Located on the oh-so-cool First Street in the Northern Quarter, HOME offers a plethora a films that you won't find in your average cinema. One of Manchester's hidden gems, this multi-purpose venue also houses a contemporary art gallery and two theatres. With comfy seating, an excellent on-site restaurant and popcorn aplenty, HOME is - quite simply - the best cinema in town!
